Drinkable

Drinkable is a campaign that promotes keeping Christchurch water clean. The campaign is targeted towards young tertiary students in Christchurch that are interested in the water that comes out of their taps. I have focused the campaign around a booth that students can go to and find out what the campaign is about.

Completed at Ara in 2017

Love worth

Love worth is a campaign that aims to get young adults to focus on how they show their love in a relationship. It aims to show that they don’t need to buy expensive gifts to show their affection, rather there are many different ways a person can show their love. I did this by creating a booklet, pamphlet, website and interactive gifts.

Completed at Ara in 2017

Banks Peninsula

Banks Peninsula is a campaign that encourages late middle aged couples to go to the many spots on the peninsula. It aims to makes the Banks Peninsula seem like a place where they can get away from their busy lives and be able to reconnect with their partner.

Completed at Ara in 2017

ECO

ECO is a redesign of the ECO site which acts as a network for environmental groups in New Zealand. For this project I focused on getting viewers to donate to the group giving basic information on what the group does.

Completed at Yoobee in 2018
